About The Competition

The Online Essay Writing Competition, organized by RP Education and Learning, is an exciting opportunity for students of Classes 4 to 8 to showcase their creativity, critical thinking, and writing skills. This event aims to inspire young learners to express their thoughts on meaningful topics, enhancing their understanding, language proficiency, and confidence. By participating, students gain a platform to share their unique ideas and perspectives while honing their writing abilities.



Organized by RP Education and Learning, a trusted name in educational initiatives, the competition emphasizes originality and authenticity, requiring essays to be free of plagiarism and AI assistance. Each submission will be evaluated on creativity, coherence, and relevance to the topic, with winners receiving well-deserved recognition. This enriching experience celebrates the power of the written word, empowering students to think critically and write effectively. Don't miss this opportunity to let your voice be heard!

Guidelines for Submission

Online Essay Writing Competition (Class 4-8)


    1. Eligibility:
    • Open to students from Class 4 to Class 8.

    2. Essay Topics:
    • According to the themes mentioned on the home page.

    3. Submission Process:
    • Guideline for submission will be provided on our WhatsApp group.
    • Use the subject line: “Essay Writing Competition [Your Name], [Class].”

    4. Essay Format:
    • Language: English
    • Length: The essay should be between 500 - 800 words.
    • Font: Times New Roman, size 12.
    • Heading: Font size 14
    • & Spacing: 1.5 line spacing.

    5. Document Requirements:
    • Save the document as a Word file or PDF.
    • Include the following details on the first page of the document:
      • Full Name
      • Class and Section
      • School Name
      • Contact Information (Email and Phone Number of Parent/Guardian)

    6. Originality:
    • The essay must be the student’s original work.
    • No use of AI tools or automated writing assistance is allowed.
    • Essays must be free of plagiarism; submissions will be checked for originality.

    7. Deadline:
    • Submit the essay before the specified deadline mentioned in the announcement. Late submissions will not be accepted.

    8. Judging Criteria:
    • Content and Creativity
    • Clarity and Structure
    • Grammar and Language
    • Relevance to the Topic

    9. Contact for Queries:
    • For any questions, email the competition team at the provided mail ID.
